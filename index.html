Some History: About Christian Alternative Rock

üé∏ What is Christian Alternative Rock?

Christian alternative rock emerged in the 1980s as a movement combining the raw energy and creativity of alternative rock with Christian themes and values. Unlike mainstream contemporary Christian music, these artists embraced the experimental sounds, guitar-driven arrangements, and honest lyricism that defined the alternative rock, punk, metal, thrash and indie genres.

Key characteristics:Introspective lyrics, and spiritual themes delivered through an indie/alternative aesthetic rather than polished pop production.

üìç Why Orange County, California?

Orange County became the unofficial epicenter of Christian alternative rock in the 1990s, with Costa Mesa specifically hosting numerous legendary venues and recording studios. Many seminal Christian alternative bands either formed in or frequently performed in Orange County.

TomCon Location: Costa Mesa, CA - the heart of where Christian alternative rock history was made, making it the perfect location for this historic first convention.
